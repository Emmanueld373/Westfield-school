Welcome To Westfield School

We are a highly motivated, hardworking school, totally committed to providing an excellent platform for learning. The warm and happy atmosphere which pervades the school is key to our success, alongside the quality of relationships and the high levels of care which exist at all levels within members of the school community.

The life of our school is driven by enthusiasm and a desire to achieve. We want to give our children a thirst for learning that continues with them throughout their future school experiences and lives.
